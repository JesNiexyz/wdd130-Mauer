Author Bio

Audrey Mauer is a fantasy writer with a passion for crafting rich worlds, intricate conspiracies, and sharp-witted characters. From high-stakes adventures in floating islands to shadowy plots lurking beneath regal courts, her stories blend mystery, action, and a touch of the unexpected. A student at BYU-Idaho, Audrey balances her love for storytelling with an interest in cybersecurity and a knack for creative problem-solving. When she’s not writing, she enjoys sketching, singing Broadway tunes, and bringing imaginative projects to life through hands-on crafting. Her works, including the short story Blackbird and Sun-Swallower’s Song, have been recognized in competitions. She is currently developing her novels Unlucky and Raging Moon.
